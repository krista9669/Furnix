Meet Our Designers

Our award-winning pieces are born from the minds of visionary creatives. Discover the passionate individuals who blend art with everyday functionality.

John Oliver
HEAD OF SEATING

With over a decade of experience in ergonomic design, John focuses on creating chairs and sofas that look as incredible as they feel. His philosophy is that comfort should never compromise style.

Annie Lilt
LIGHTING SPECIALIST

Annie brings spaces to life through illumination. Inspired by Scandinavian minimalism, her lighting fixtures are designed to cast warm, inviting ambiances in modern homes.

Frank Daniel
WOODCRAFT MASTER

Frank's expertise lies in sustainably sourced timber. He designs our tables and bedroom sets with a deep respect for the natural grain, ensuring every piece is entirely unique.
