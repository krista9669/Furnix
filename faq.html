Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers regarding our products, shipping logistics, and return policies.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return window for all our standard furniture pieces. If you are not completely satisfied, you can return the item in its original packaging for a full refund, minus standard return shipping costs.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we provide fast and free shipping within the continental United States and Canada on orders over $150. We are actively expanding our logistics network to support global orders in the near future.

How do I maintain my wooden furniture?

To maintain the premium finish, simply wipe the surface with a soft, slightly damp cloth. Avoid harsh chemical cleaners and always place coasters under hot or wet items to prevent water rings and heat damage over time.

Are your materials sustainably sourced?

Absolutely. We are deeply committed to environmental responsibility. All our wood is FSC-certified, and our upholstery fabrics are woven from organic or recycled fibers wherever possible.
